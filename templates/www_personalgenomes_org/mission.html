Our Mission

Our mission is to make a wide spectrum of data about humans accessible and actionable to increase biological literacy and improve human health. We are a nonprofit organization working with leading scientists and volunteers to generate, aggregate and interpret human biological and trait data on an unprecedented scale using open-source, open-access and open-consent frameworks. Our efforts are informed by values encouraging greater transparency and collaboration between researchers and participants.
