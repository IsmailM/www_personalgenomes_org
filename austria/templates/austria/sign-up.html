{% trans "Pre-registration as a Volunteer" %}

{% blocktrans %} Genom Austria is accepting pre-registration by volunteers who would like to analyze their personal genomes and to make them publicly available as part of Genom Austria. Pre-registering as a volunteer does not guarantee participation, as there will initially be sufficient resources available for only a handful of participants. Furthermore, in order to meet the highest ethical standards, all volunteers have to confirm in several steps that they understand the implications of making their personal genomes publicly available. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Requirements for volunteers:" %}
  • {% trans "At least 18 years of age" %}
  • {% blocktrans %} No citizenship requirement, but preference will be given to volunteers who live in Austria or have other connections to Austria {% endblocktrans %}
