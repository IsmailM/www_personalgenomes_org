{% trans "Pre-registration as a Volunteer" %}

{% blocktrans %} Genom Austria is accepting pre-registration by volunteers who would like to analyze their personal genomes and make them publicly available as part of Genom Austria. Pre-registering as a volunteer does not guarantee participation because in the initial phase of the project there will be sufficient resources for only a handful of participants. Furthermore, in order to meet the highest ethical standards, all volunteers will have to confirm at several stages that they understand the implications of making their personal genome publicly available. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Requirements for volunteers:" %}
  • {% trans "At least 18 years of age" %}
  • {% blocktrans %} No citizenship requirement, but preference will be given to volunteers who live in Austria or have other connections to Austria {% endblocktrans %}
