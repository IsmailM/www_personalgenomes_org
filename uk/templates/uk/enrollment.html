Enrollment Process and Requirements

Enrolling in the PGP-UK is an online process. Individuals considering participation should closely review our study guide and consent forms. To qualify for enrollment, prospective participants must complete an online exam to demonstrate their comprehension of the risks and protocols associated with being a member of the PGP-UK.

Requirements:

  • Willing to share genetic, health and trait data in a public and non-anonymous manner.
  • United Kingdom citizen or permanent resident.
  • At least 21 years of age.
  • Capable of giving autonomous consent. We do not permit enrollment through legal guardianship, for example.
  • Identical (monozygotic) twins are welcome, but both twins must complete the enrollment process to be eligible.
